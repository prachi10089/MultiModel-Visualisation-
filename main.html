Multimodal Visualization

Our website serves as a comprehensive hub for data visualization and analysis. It offers users a range of tools to explore their data effectively. Upon uploading their datasets, users can easily generate various visualizations to identify patterns and trends. Automated plot generation simplifies the process, while a summary option distills key insights for quick understanding. Additionally, a recommendation system suggests suitable plots based on data characteristics, enhancing user experience. For those preferring alternative learning methods, the platform offers video summaries for a more engaging experience. An OpenAI chatbot provides instant support, ensuring users can navigate the platform effortlessly and get answers to their queries promptly.
